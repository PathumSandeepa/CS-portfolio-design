    Hello! My name is Pathum Sandeepa Pandithaarachchi, and I'm a passionate software developer with a strong focus on innovation and problem-solving. I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Computer Science from the University of Westminster, through the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT) in Sri Lanka. Alongside my studies, I work full-time at Xgen Group Private Limited, a dynamic software company with roots in both Australia and Sri Lanka. Xgen specializes in providing innovative banking solutions, allowing me to gain hands-on experience in the software industry while expanding my technical skills.

    My technical toolkit includes languages like PHP, Laravel, and JavaScript frameworks such as Vue.js, with some experience in Next.js and React. I'm eager to deepen my knowledge in these areas and explore new technologies that can make a real impact. I’m fascinated by the endless possibilities of technology and am constantly seeking to learn and innovate.

    Outside of work and studies, I enjoy experimenting with new tech, reading about the latest trends in the industry, and working on personal projects. I'm driven by a desire to solve real-world problems and am always looking for opportunities to apply my skills in meaningful ways. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work!
